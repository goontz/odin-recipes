Sausage and eggs

Super simple S + E

This is made to be quick, easy, nutritious and healthy

Ingredients

  • 4-6 farm fresh eggs
  • 4-6 grass feed organic beef sausages no additives

Instructions

  1. Heat webber bbq or cast iron pan to a high heat
  2. Throw sausages on and cook 8 minutes a side
  3. Cook eggs in remaining sausage fat, fry until desired level
  4. Enjoy your perfectly balanced seed oil free breakfast
