Bacon and eggs

Super simple B + E

This is made to be quick, easy, nutritious and healthy

Ingredients

  • 4-6 farm fresh eggs
  • 4-6 rashers of nitrate free bacon

Instructions

  1. Heat webber bbq or cast iron pan to a medium heat
  2. Throw the bacon on and cook until desired level of crisp
  3. Cook eggs in remaining bacon fat, fry until desired level
  4. Enjoy your perfectly balanced seed oil free breakfast
